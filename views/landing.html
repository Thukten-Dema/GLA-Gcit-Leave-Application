Through this platform, Gcit students and staff will be able to submit online leave application making it easier and save time. It will be approved by administration and SSO based on the type of leave category. Staff leave application wil be handled by administration only whereas students leave will be handled both by administration and SSO. So, join GCITLA to easily submit your leave application.

To simplify leave request processing for GCIT students, SSO, and administration through an intuitive web application, reducing paperwork, saving time, and enhancing the overall efficiency of the leave management process.

To create a seamless digital leave management system at GCIT, enabling students to effortlessly request leave and SSO/administration to efficiently approve or reject requests, promoting a more productive and balanced campus life for all.
